Who
We Are

Check la Lage – “Let’s Check it Out” if you blend a little English, French and German – is an improv theatre group of 8 improvisers. We bring together a rotating cast of international improvisers to deliver dynamic, entertaining performances on Berlin stages — at least once a month.

Our shows are both a creative playground and a space for growth. With every performance, we gain experience, refine our craft, and experiment with new formats. We stay open to fresh ideas and creative risks, treating each show as a chance to learn, sharpen our skills, and connect with audiences — whether they're improv aficionados or first-time viewers.

Above all, we're here to have some fun.

Founded by Lorenz von Römer & Stéphane Piette
